Legal Basis

SafeCloak consent tooling supports compliance workflows by providing:

  • Explicit informed consent capture
  • Cryptographic timestamps (SHA-256)
  • Tamper-evident hash verification
  • Consent withdrawal mechanism
  • Machine-readable export (JSON/CSV)
  • Purpose-of-processing field

Note: this tool supports compliance practices but does not replace legal counsel.
